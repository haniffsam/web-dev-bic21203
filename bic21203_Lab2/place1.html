Place 1: Osaka, Japan

Things to do in Osaka, Japan:

  1. Eat my way through Dotonbori - Takoyaki, okonomiyaki, and all the street food!
  2. Visit Osaka Castle - See the famous samurai fortress and take photos
  3. Ride the Tempozan Ferris Wheel - Get epic views of Osaka Bay
  4. Try fresh sushi at Kuromon Market - Experience the famous fish market
  5. Explore Shinsekai at night - See the retro district with Tsutenkaku Tower
  6. Find unique anime goods in Nipponbashi - Hunt for collectibles
