Place 2: Phuket, Thailand

Things to do in Phuket, Thailand:

  1. Relax on Patong Beach - Sunbathe, swim and enjoy the vibrant beach scene
  2. Visit an ethical elephant sanctuary - Feed and bathe rescued elephants
  3. Island hopping tour - Visit Phi Phi Islands, James Bond Island and Maya Bay
  4. Watch sunset at Promthep Cape - See the most famous sunset spot in Phuket
  5. Shop at Old Phuket Town - Buy souvenirs and see colorful Sino-Portuguese buildings
  6. Get a traditional Thai massage - Experience authentic relaxation
